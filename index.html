About Us

At Wes Cafe, we're more than just a place to grab a bite or a cup of coffee – we're a community. Since 2023, we've been serving up delicious meals and warm hospitality to our guests in Ambalangoda. Our commitment to quality ingredients and innovative flavors sets us apart, but it's our cozy atmosphere and friendly staff that keep people coming back. Whether you're here for a quick pick-me-up or to linger over brunch with friends, we invite you to make yourself at home at Wes Cafe.
